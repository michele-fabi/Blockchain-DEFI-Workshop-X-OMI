Presentations

Regular presentations will last 30 minutes each, with 5 minutes of Q&A.

Please time yourself for a 25-minute talk.

The keynote presentation will last an hour long, with 45 minutes of presentation and 10-15 minutes of Q&A.

For presenters: before your panel starts, please send your slides to michele.fabi@ensae.fr attached to an email entitled “SLIDES-WORKSHOP”.
